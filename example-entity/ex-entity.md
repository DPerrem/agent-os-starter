## What Acme Does

Acme Co is a small consulting firm helping mid-market manufacturers digitise their operations. Two-person team. Project-based engagements ranging $20-100k. Founded 2023.

The plain-English description: *we walk into factories that still run on spreadsheets and help them adopt modern operational tools without breaking what already works.*

---

## Who It Serves

**Primary:** Operations directors at manufacturers with $5M-$50M revenue, 20-200 employees, who know they're behind on digital but don't know where to start.

**Secondary:** Family-owned businesses going through a generational handover, where the next generation wants to modernise.

**Not a fit:** Pure software companies, large enterprise, anyone wanting a cheap automation hack.

## Offer

### Lead service: Operations Modernisation Sprint

A 6-week structured engagement:
1. **Week 1** — Operational audit. Site visit, interviews, map current workflows.
2. **Weeks 2-3** — Design future state. Tool recommendations, change plan.
3. **Weeks 4-5** — Implementation support. We don't deploy; we coach the client's team.
4. **Week 6** — Handover, training, 30-day review plan.

**Price:** $35k fixed.
**Deliverable:** Operational playbook + tool stack + 90-day roadmap.

### Secondary service: Tool Selection Consulting

Lighter-touch — help the client pick between options for a specific tool category (ERP, MRP, scheduling). 2-week engagement, $8k.

### What we don't do
- Build software.
- Long-term retainers.
- Vendor reselling.

---

## Voice & Tone

- **Direct.** Manufacturers don't have time for jargon. We speak plain.
- **Pragmatic.** Pragmatism > ideology. Best tool is the one the team will actually use.
- **Respectful of legacy.** "Just rip it out" is wrong 80% of the time.
- **Confident, not cocky.** We've done this 30+ times. We say so without bragging.

Phrases we use: *"start with the bottleneck", "the goal is fewer surprises", "good enough beats perfect that nobody adopts"*.

Phrases we don't: *"digital transformation" (overused), "synergy", "best-in-class"*.
