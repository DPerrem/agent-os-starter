---

## How AI Should Use This

When I ask AI to write "in the style of [person]" or "matching the [person] voice," the AI should:

- Apply the voice characteristics above.
- Use signature phrases sparingly (sound like them, don't impersonate).
- Avoid the "never do" list rigidly.
- Default to one of the three best examples as a structural reference.
